### Equations
**Density**
$p = \frac{m}{v}$
**Density Uncertainty**
$(\frac{\delta p}{p_0})^2 = (\frac{\delta M}{M_0})^2 + (\frac{\delta W}{W_0})^2 + (\frac{\delta L}{L_0})^2 + (\frac{\delta H}{H_0})^2$
Where:
- $\delta p = p_{uncertaincy}$ 
- $p_0 = density$
- $\delta M = mass_{uncertaincy}$ 
- $M_0 = mass$
- $\delta W = width_{uncertaincy}$ 
- $W_0 = width$
- $\delta L = length_{uncertaincy}$ 
- $L_0 = length$
- $\delta H = height_{uncertaincy}$ 
- $H_0 = height$
#### Aluminium
**Density**
$p = \frac{0.5677}{0.00124 \times 0.457 \times 0.372}$

**Density Uncertainty**
$\delta p = 2693.011 \times \sqrt{(\frac{5 \times 10^{-6}}{0.5677})^2 + (\frac{5\times10^{-6}}{0.00129})^2 + (\frac{5\times10^{-4}}{0.457})^2 + (\frac{5\times10^{-4}}{0.372})^2} = 12$

$\therefore p = (2693 \pm 12)kg/m^3$
